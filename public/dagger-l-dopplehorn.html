Technical and Historical Notes

The GAT-02L2 Dagger L could be outfitted for various mission roles through the use of Striker packs. The AQM/E-M11 Doppelhorn Striker pack was a large dual recoilless cannon that was capable of inflicting heavy damage on a battleship or mobile suit. On October 2, C.E. 73, several Doppelhorn Dagger Ls attached to the Phantom Pain ship Girty Lue attacked the spaceport at ZAFT’s Armory One PLANT during an operation to steal ZAFT’s latest Gundam model mobile suits.
