Technical and Historical Notes

The GAT-02L2+AQM/E-A4E1 Jet Dagger L used a Jet Striker pack similar to the original AQM/E-X01 Aile Striker pack developed for the GAT-X105 Strike Gundam. In contrast to the Aile Striker, the Jet Striker allowed atmospheric flight and featured numerous launchers for rockets, air-to-surface and air-to-air missiles.
